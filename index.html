Most Recent Academic Works

In August 2021 I completed my third year dissertation that used LiDAR data to determine the extent of carbon sequestration in response to anthropogenic pressures of urban settlements and coniferous plantations. My method used open source data from Natural England and the Environment Agency to conclude no significant difference was recorded between the pressured sites and the controls. I hope to one day continue my research into widescale carbon monitoring in forested environments by calibrating the biomass caluclation used, and attempting to reproduce the experiment to verify my findings.
